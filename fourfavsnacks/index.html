ABOUT THIS SITE

This site showcases four of my favorite Hawaii snacks from my childhood. I’m sure these snacks are available in the mainland but to me these snack items are endearing to Hawai’is culture. This site will give you more insight on these snacks and maybe hunch people to try them if they have not already. Each snack will tell you what each snack is made of, different varieties of the selected snack, and how I like to eat each snack.
